Stargate

Stargate is a brick device that a ring-shaped portal that enables rapid transportation to other stargates located cosmic distances away.

History

Discovered in Giza in 1928, an Egyptologist enables use of the Stargate when he recognizes that symbols on the cover stones of the Stargate are asterisms used in a three dimensional coordinate system.

A team is ordered to step through the Stargate and identify potential military threats on the other side.

What?

Stargate Atlantis explore distant planets in the Pegasus Galaxy. They use an alien device known as a Stargate that was built millions of years ago by an advanced race of humans known as the Ancients.

Where?

The expedition is based in the Lost City of Atlantis on the planet "Lantea".

When?

The city was built millions of years ago and abandoned 10,000 years ago by the Ancients. Five to ten million years ago, due to a plague in the Milky Way Galaxy, they were forced to flee to the Pegasus Galaxy, and there they seeded life on hundreds of worlds as they had done to Earth in the Milky Way.

Who?

Stargate Command sends an international team to investigate the outpost.

Against?

After encountering a powerful enemy known as the Wraith and going to war with them for one hundred years, the Ancients ultimately lost and were forced to submerge their city beneath Lantea's ocean, which, in the Stargate universe, is the source of the Greek myth of the Lost City of Atlantis.
